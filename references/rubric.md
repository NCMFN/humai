# Rubric

Validation must pass the following checks:
* Sentence length standard deviation ≥ 7 words across each paragraph.
* Paragraph-opening lexical diversity ≥ 80% across the document.
* Zero instances of Category A taboo phrases in the final output.
* 100% preservation of all locked numerical and citation content.
* Consistent academic tone (third-person academic tone by default).
* Logical flow without over-signposting (remove "The remainder of this paper is organized as follows").
* No redundancy within sections.

The final output format must strictly include three distinct sections:
1. 'Draft rewrite'
2. 'Anti-AI pass analysis' (bullet points identifying AI-generated characteristics)
3. 'Final rewrite' (with LaTeX-preserved structure)
