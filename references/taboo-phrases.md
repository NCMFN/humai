# Taboo Phrases List

This is a categorized list of AI-isms and formulaic phrasing that must be eliminated entirely from any humanized output.

## Tier 1: Immediate Flags (Banned Words)
- Leverage
- Robust
- Delve
- Testament (as in "a testament to")
- Showcase (as a verb, e.g., "showcases")
- Underscore (as a verb, e.g., "underscores")
- Highlight (as a superficial verb, e.g., "highlighting the need")
- Reshape (e.g., "reshaping the landscape")
- Evolve (e.g., "rapidly evolving landscape")
- Tapestry
- Myriad

## Tier 2: Significance Inflation
- "Marking a pivotal moment"
- "In today's rapidly evolving technological landscape"
- "Groundbreaking tools"
- "Nestled at the intersection of"
- "Transformative potential"

## Tier 3: Formulaic Templates
- "Not just X, but Y"
- "From X to Y" (when used to list random examples)
- "Despite challenges... continues to thrive"
- "The rule of three" (X, Y, and Z—cut one or make it a real list)

## Tier 4: Superficial '-ing' Connections
- "Symbolizing..."
- "Reflecting..."
- "Contributing to..."
- "Fostering..."
- "Ensuring that..."

## Tier 5: Copula Avoidance
Replace these with direct verbs (is/are/has/does/makes):
- "Serves as"
- "Functions as"
- "Stands as"
- "Acts as"
- "Boasts"
- Features (when used as a verb)

## Tier 6: Chatbot Artifacts & Sycophancy
- "I hope this helps!"
- "Let me know if..."
- "Great question!"
- "You're absolutely right!"
- "In conclusion..."
- "The future looks bright."
- "Exciting times lie ahead."

## Tier 7: Hedging & Filler
- "In order to" (use "To")
- "Due to the fact that" (use "Because")
- "It is important to note that"
- "Could potentially possibly be argued"
- "While specific details are limited..."
