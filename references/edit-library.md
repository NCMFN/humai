# Edit Library

Before/After examples of common transformations to fix AI-generated text.

## 1. Significance Inflation

**After:** was established in 1989 to collect regional statistics.

## 2. Promotional Language
**Before:** Nestled within the breathtaking region of Gonder, Alamata stands as a vibrant town with stunning natural beauty.
**After:** Alamata is a town in the Gonder region known for its weekly market.

## 3. Vague Attributions
**Before:** Experts believe it plays a crucial role in the regional ecosystem.
**After:** The Haolai River supports several endemic fish species, according to a 2019 survey.

## 4. AI Vocabulary
**Before:** Additionally, an enduring testament to Italian colonial influence is the widespread adoption of pasta, showcasing the intricate tapestry of the diet.
**After:** Pasta dishes, introduced during Italian colonization, remain common.

## 5. Copula Avoidance
**Before:** Gallery 825 serves as LAAA's exhibition space and boasts over 3,000 square feet.
**After:** Gallery 825 is LAAA's exhibition space. The gallery has 3,000 square feet.

## 6. Negative Parallelism
**Before:** It's not just about the beat riding under the vocals; it's part of the aggression.
**After:** The heavy beat adds to the aggressive tone.

## 7. Em Dash Overuse
**Before:** The term is promoted by Dutch institutions—not by the people—yet this mislabeling continues—even in official documents.
**After:** The term is promoted by Dutch institutions, not by the people. Yet this mislabeling continues in official documents.

## 8. Filler Phrases
**Before:** Due to the fact that it was raining, the event was canceled in order to ensure safety.
**After:** The event was canceled because of the rain.
