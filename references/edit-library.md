# Edit Library

## Example 1: Inflated Significance
**AI Pattern:** "The study is a testament to the robust framework and sheds light on the multifaceted nature of..."
**Transformation:** "The study demonstrates the framework's reliability and identifies three distinct factors in..."
**Rationale:** Removed "testament," "robust framework," "sheds light on," and "multifaceted." Replaced abstract verbs with concrete ones ("demonstrates", "identifies"). Quantified the findings ("three distinct factors").

## Example 2: Hollow Intensifiers
**AI Pattern:** "The proposed method significantly improved the performance of the system."
**Transformation:** "The proposed method reduced inference time by 14%."
**Rationale:** Removed the unquantified "significantly improved." Replaced it with the exact locked fact ("reduced inference time by 14%").

## Example 3: Passive/Formal Overuse
**AI Pattern:** "It is worth noting that the results were comprehensively analyzed by the research team."
**Transformation:** "We analyzed the results to determine..." or "The analysis showed..." (depending on first vs third person).
**Rationale:** Removed the filler transition "It is worth noting that" and the passive construction. Replaced "comprehensively analyzed" with a direct action.

## Example 4: The "Delve"
**AI Pattern:** "In this section, we delve into the intricate details of the methodology."
**Transformation:** "This section details the methodology."
**Rationale:** Removed "delve into" and "intricate details." The revised sentence is direct and maintains academic register without AI fluff.

## Example 5: Sentence Length Monotony
**AI Pattern:** "The data was collected over six months. The sensors recorded temperature and humidity. The readings were stored in a database." (All 6-7 words)
**Transformation:** "Over six months, the sensors continuously recorded temperature and humidity. These readings were then aggregated and stored in a central database for analysis." (11 words, 12 words)
**Rationale:** Broke the monotonous rhythm by combining sentences and introducing variance in length and structure.

## Example 6: "The Remainder of this Paper"
**AI Pattern:** "The remainder of this paper is organized as follows. Section 2 discusses..."
**Transformation:** (Remove entirely or integrate smoothly). "Following a review of related work in Section 2, we introduce..."
**Rationale:** Eliminated the template structure, creating a more natural transition.
